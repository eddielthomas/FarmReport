# Equipment Intelligence — Report Family Recipe Matrix

## Persona & the fear it answers

The buyer here is the **Maintenance Manager / Fleet & Operations Director** at a large grower, co-op, or processor fleet (Dole/Del Monte harvest crews, a Cargill/Tyson logistics yard, a regional farm-manager running 40+ machines). Their career hangs on a single word: **uptime**. When a combine throws a hydraulic code at 2am during a 10-day harvest window, or the sprayer fleet isn't calibrated when the disease pressure spikes, they are the person who explains to the CEO why the crop rotted in the field and the Walmart delivery slipped. This family answers the fears: *"Will my fleet actually be running when the window opens? Which machine is about to strand a crew mid-field? Am I burning cash on fuel, idle hours, and emergency parts? Should I repair this machine or replace it — and can I defend that number to finance?"* Every report converts raw telematics into a **production-risk and dollar decision**, not a dashboard of engine hours.

> **Reality check:** Report.Farm delegates EO/ML to the AlphaGeo gateway, but the gateway does **not** ingest machine telemetry today. Almost every report in this family is gated on an **EXT-DATA** equipment-telematics connector (AEMP / ISO 15143-3, JDLink, AGCO Fuse, CNH AFS, raw CAN/ISOBUS). Location and utilization-by-field additionally reuse the LIVE `/api/gis/parcel/delineate` boundaries for the geofence overlay. Predictive reports layer a **NEW-MODEL** failure/RUL learner on top of that feed. We are honest: no telemetry connector = no data, so these are EXT-DATA first, model second.
