ROLE · GROWERS & FARM OPERATORS

See the field the day it starts to slip — not at harvest.

Crop stress, a stuck pivot, or an early disease cluster can cost a whole season, and most of it starts long before it's visible from the cab. Report.Farm uses independent, multi-source satellite imagery and AI to track NDVI, soil moisture, and stress across every field, then hands your team prioritized, field-ready scouting tasks — not heatmaps. Every finding arrives explained, with a financial number and the confidence behind it.

  • ⊕Reports plus urgent alertsScheduled field reports and event-driven alerts, routed to your farm-management system.
