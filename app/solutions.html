SOLUTION LINE · 01

Crop health & NDVI.

Crop stress shows up from orbit before it shows up on a windshield tour. Report.Farm tracks NDVI, EVI, canopy trend, and the crop-stage timeline against each field's own history, separates real stress from cloud and seasonal noise, maps it to management zones, and turns it into scouting tasks your agronomist can act on. You see ranked, explained findings — not raw imagery.

  • ◔Mapped to management zonesStress located precisely enough to send a scout to the right corner of the right field — with a confidence ranking, not a guess.
  • ≋Real stress, not cloudTrue canopy decline separated from cloud, haze, and seasonal green-up, so you chase agronomy — not artifacts.
  • ⊞Scouting tasks you can routeFindings become prioritized field tasks in the portal, the reports, or your farm-management system.
  • ⊕Judged against your own normalA multi-season baseline per field means anomalies are measured against how that field actually behaves.

SOLUTION LINE · 05

Irrigation & water intelligence.

Water is where a good season is won or lost — and where the most expensive surprises hide. Report.Farm reads soil-moisture proxies from radar, detects irrigation outages and waterlogging, and watches the ponds and canals your operation depends on, so a stuck pivot or a failing line surfaces in a report or an alert instead of a stunted strip of crop.

  • ⊞Soil-moisture proxiesSAR-based moisture signals, field by field, that see through cloud and read the ground, not just the canopy.
  • ≋Irrigation anomaly detectionMissed passes, stuck pivots, and dry strips flagged against the pattern each field usually shows.
  • ⌂Waterlogging & flood flagsStanding water and saturation mapped early, so you act before roots and yield pay for it.
  • ◉Pond & canal monitoringWater-body levels and canal integrity watched over time, with change surfaced against baseline.
