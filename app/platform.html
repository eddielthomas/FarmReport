WHAT YOU GET

Signals in. A clear decision out. On a schedule you set.

A clean line from open satellite observation to a report your team can act on — with the confidence, the likely cost, and the recommended next step attached.

01 · Ingest

Continuous eyes overhead

Satellite imagery, radar, weather and optional field sensors flow in automatically on every revisit — no one has to remember to pull data or check the field.

02 · Detect

What changed, and where

Indices and change detection surface emerging crop stress, water anomalies, disease clusters and land change across each zone — ranked so you work the highest-impact issue first.

03 · Explain

Why, and what it's worth

Each finding comes with a likely cause, a confidence score, and an estimate of the revenue or cost at stake — so it's a decision, not a raw pixel.

04 · Report & alert

Delivered on your terms

Scheduled reports land on your cadence; urgent alerts fire only when confidence and business impact clear your thresholds — with every finding recorded and reproducible.

FARM INTELLIGENCE

See the change before it costs you the yield.

Most farm software shows you a dashboard of what you already knew. Report.Farm is built to tell you what changed since last week and what it's likely to mean — so the work moves from reacting after harvest to acting mid-season, while there's still time to protect the crop.

  • ◉Standards-first Earth observationRuns on open Sentinel-2 and Landsat optical imagery for a low-cost, high-frequency baseline — escalating to commercial imagery only when a finding warrants it.
  • ◎Sees through cloud and nightSentinel-1 C-band radar isn't stopped by cloud or darkness, so water and structure signals stay consistent across seasons and weather.
  • ⊕Indices, change & a digital twinNDVI, EVI and multi-date change detection feed a living twin of your parcels, zones and assets — every finding ranked by likely business impact, not by whichever pixel moved most.
  • ⚞Your fields, your zone intentDraw the ground you care about — a block, an orchard, a ranch, a sourcing region — and tell each zone its intent, so a barn that should never hold water alerts differently than a wetland.

TRUST BY DESIGN

Built for the people who carry the yield and the budget.

Growers, farm managers and sourcing teams don't just need a pretty map — they need findings they can act on and defend, backed by evidence and a record of how confident the system was. Governance and provenance are first-class parts of the platform, not an afterthought.

⬚

Multi-tenant isolation

Every operation's fields, findings and history are strictly isolated from every other tenant — enforced at the data layer, so one customer can never see another's farms.

⊞

Role-based access

Surfaces are governed by role. Owners, agronomists, farm managers and outside partners each get a scoped view — see exactly what the job needs, nothing more.

▤

Audit trail

Every observation, query and action is recorded to a durable audit trail — so what the platform saw, when, and who acted on it is never in question.

⎙

Evidence-grade reports

Reports export as defensible packages — before/after imagery, indices, confidence and provenance — the kind of record you can put in front of a lender, an insurer or a buyer.

◷

Standards-first integration

Open standards come first — STAC, OGC APIs, COG and GeoParquet for geospatial; ISOBUS/ISOXML and MQTT for machinery and sensors — so findings flow into the tools you already run.

⟳

Continuously learning

Your field feedback and real outcomes feed back in, so the intelligence sharpens over time against the ground you actually farm — highest-value labels first.

API · WEBHOOKS

Wire Report.Farm into your stack.

The platform is API-first and standards-first. Findings, reports and audit records are reachable through a versioned REST surface secured with token auth and per-tenant scopes, with geospatial payloads served as GeoJSON. Events emit as webhooks so your downstream systems — FMIS, GIS, ERP — react in real time.

  • ⊞Versioned REST APIA stable /api/v1 surface so integrations don't break under you.
  • ⊕Scoped, tenant-aware authTokens are tenant-scoped with explicit grants per integration — least privilege by default.
  • ◎Signed webhook eventsReliable, signed event delivery with retries, so nothing silently goes missing.
