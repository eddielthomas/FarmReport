Who we plan for

Built for the people who carry the yield.

Whether you farm thousands of acres, steward the land, or source from it, the question is the same: what changed out there, and what is it going to cost? We answer it while there's still time to act.

Farmers & growers

Row crops, orchards & vineyards

Watch every zone for crop stress, water anomalies and disease across land too large to walk. We surface change before it erodes yield.

  • Crop stress & disease clusters, caught early
  • Irrigation & water anomalies within days
  • Early warning that protects the crop and the season
Landowners & estates

Ranches, plantations & estates

Protect water, soil and property value across the whole property line — and keep an evidence-grade record you can put in front of a lender, insurer or buyer.

  • Whole-property coverage from above
  • Water loss, seepage & land movement
  • Condition data for lending & underwriting
Agribusiness & buyers

Buyers · traders · lenders & insurers

Monitor a portfolio of suppliers and sourcing regions from one place — early warning on drought, flood and yield shortfall before it reaches your contracts.

  • Portfolio view across suppliers & regions
  • Early warning on drought, flood & shortfall
  • Independent condition data for sourcing decisions
